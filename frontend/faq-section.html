Mushroom spawn seeds are not seeds in the traditional sense — they are sterilized grain or substrate inoculated with mushroom mycelium. Think of spawn as the "root system" of a mushroom, ready to colonize fresh substrate and produce mushrooms. Unlike vegetable seeds that contain a dormant plant embryo, spawn contains living fungal tissue that must be handled with care. Sporekart produces high-quality spawn seeds using carefully selected grain and controlled sterilization processes. Our spawn is available in various grain types including wheat, bajra, and sorghum, optimized for different mushroom varieties. When you buy mushroom spawn seeds online from Sporekart, you receive actively growing, laboratory-tested mycelium ready for cultivation.

Proper storage of mushroom spawn seeds is essential to maintain their viability. Upon receiving your spawn from Sporekart, store it in a cool, dark, and well-ventilated place at a temperature between 2°C and 8°C (refrigerator temperature) if you plan to use it within a week. For short-term storage of 1–3 days, a cool, shaded area away from direct sunlight is sufficient. Never freeze the spawn, as freezing damages the mycelium. Keep the spawn bags in their original packaging and avoid opening them until you are ready to use them. If you have ordered oyster mushroom spawn seeds for immediate use, we recommend using them as soon as possible for the best results. Always inspect the spawn for any signs of contamination (unusual colours or foul odour) before use. For detailed storage instructions specific to each variety, refer to the product guide included with your order.

The shelf life of mushroom spawn seeds depends on the variety and storage conditions. Generally, Sporekart's spawn seeds remain viable for 15 to 30 days when stored under optimal conditions (2–8°C). At room temperature, the shelf life reduces to approximately 7 to 10 days. Oyster mushroom spawn tends to have a shorter usable window compared to milky or button mushroom spawn. We recommend using the spawn within the first week of receipt for the best colonization rates and maximum yield. We dispatch spawn seeds in a fresh, actively growing state to ensure you receive the highest quality product. If you are planning a large-scale mushroom cultivation project, coordinate your spawn delivery schedule with our team to align with your substrate preparation timeline.

Dry mushrooms from Sporekart should be stored in an airtight container in a cool, dry, and dark place away from direct sunlight and moisture. When stored properly at room temperature (below 30°C), dried mushrooms remain good for 6 to 12 months without significant loss of flavour or nutritional value. For extended storage, you can keep them in the refrigerator, where they can last up to 18 months. Always ensure the container is completely sealed after each use. If you notice any moisture inside the container, remove the mushrooms and dry them again in a dehydrator or in the sun before returning to storage. To use dry mushrooms, simply soak them in warm water for 20–30 minutes before cooking — the soaking water can also be used as a flavourful broth in soups and gravies.
