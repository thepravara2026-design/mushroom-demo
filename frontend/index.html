Our Story

Shriyap Enterprise – A Journey of Passion, Perseverance, and Women Entrepreneurship

Founded in 2016 by Smt. Jyothi R in the rural village of Basapura, Davangere, Shriyap Enterprise stands today as a remarkable example of how passion for agriculture can evolve into a successful entrepreneurial venture.

What began as the dream of a cultivation enthusiast gradually transformed into a thriving enterprise dedicated to mushroom cultivation, spawn production, farmer empowerment, and sustainable agriculture. Driven by determination and guided by a vision to create opportunities through innovative farming practices, Smt. Jyothi R embarked on her entrepreneurial journey with a strong commitment to quality and customer trust.

A significant pillar in this journey has been the valuable guidance and support of Sri Chethan of Tanvi Biotechnologies, Davangere, whose mentorship helped shape the foundation and growth of the enterprise.

In its early stages, Shriyap Enterprise entered the e-commerce ecosystem under the brand Sporekart Spawn Seeds, focusing on high-quality mushroom spawn production and distribution. Through consistent quality standards, customer satisfaction, and technical excellence, Sporekart Spawns quickly earned recognition as a trusted brand among mushroom cultivators across India.

Today, Sporekart products are available on leading agricultural and e-commerce platforms such as Amazon, Flipkart, Agribegri, Kisan e-Shop, and several other marketplaces, reaching thousands of farmers, entrepreneurs, and cultivation enthusiasts nationwide.

Building upon its online success, Shriyap Enterprise expanded its presence into offline markets, creating a strong distribution network for both fresh and dry mushrooms. The enterprise has established itself as a reliable supplier of mushroom cultivation solutions, serving farmers, agripreneurs, educational institutions, and aspiring business owners.

Over the years, Shriyap Enterprise has diversified its offerings to include:

  • Supply of all varieties of mushroom spawns
  • Mushroom cultivation units
  • Spawn production units
  • Cultivation infrastructure and equipment
  • Technical training programs
  • End-to-end cultivation guidance and support
  • Farmer and entrepreneur mentoring

Continuing its commitment to innovation, the enterprise has now ventured into the emerging field of Medicinal Mushroom Cultivation, providing specialized cultivation support, training, and technical assistance for medicinal mushroom varieties that offer significant health and commercial value.

At its heart, Shriyap Enterprise represents the strength of women entrepreneurship in rural India. The journey of Smt. Jyothi R demonstrates that with dedication, continuous learning, and the right guidance, women can successfully lead enterprises that create economic opportunities, promote sustainable agriculture, and inspire future generations.

Today, Shriyap Enterprise continues its mission of empowering cultivators, supporting agricultural innovation, and delivering quality products and services that contribute to the growth of India's mushroom industry.

"From a cultivation enthusiast to a successful entrepreneur, the journey of Shriyap Enterprise is a story of vision, resilience, and empowerment."

Our Story

From a Backyard Experiment
to a Pan-India Movement

Sporekart began in 2019 in a small shed in Davangere, Karnataka, with a single tray of oyster mushroom spawn and a simple belief — that mushroom cultivation could change rural livelihoods. What started as a weekend experiment quickly turned into a mission to make premium spawn, fresh mushrooms, and cultivation knowledge accessible to every farmer, hobbyist, and entrepreneur across India.

Today, we work with over 500 trained farmers across 50+ cities, supplying lab-tested spawn, dried mushrooms, and complete grow kits — backed by hands-on training and lifetime support.
